# Australia ESG Requirements

Australia is introducing **mandatory climate-related financial disclosures** from 2025, aligned with ISSB standards, making it one of the first countries to mandate IFRS S1 and S2.

**Group 2 (FY 2026):** Other large companies  
**Group 3 (FY 2027):** Medium-sized companies

## Why Australia ESG Matters

Australia is a major **resources exporter** and early adopter of mandatory climate disclosure. The regulations will significantly impact mining, energy, and agricultural sectors globally.
