## K-Taxonomy

The **Korean Green Classification System** defines green activities across six environmental objectives, similar to EU Taxonomy:

1. Greenhouse gas reduction  
2. Climate change adaptation  
3. Sustainable water management  
4. Circular economy transition  
5. Pollution prevention  
6. Biodiversity protection

## Why Korea ESG Matters

South Korea is the world's **10th-largest economy** with major global corporations (Samsung, Hyundai, LG). Korea's ESG regulations influence global supply chains and technology sectors.
