# Product Safety & Quality

**Product safety** ensures products do not pose unreasonable risks to consumers during normal use.

## Regulatory Framework

**Consumer Product Safety** — CPSC (US), CPSA (Canada), General Product Safety Directive (EU)  
**Food Safety** — FDA (US), EFSA (EU), Codex Alimentarius (international)  
**Pharmaceutical Safety** — FDA, EMA, ICH guidelines

## Product Safety Management

### Design Phase

**Risk Assessment** — Identify potential hazards in product design  
**Safety Standards** — Comply with applicable safety standards  
**Testing** — Conduct safety testing before market release

### Manufacturing Phase

**Quality Control** — In-process inspections and testing  
**Traceability** — Track materials and components  
**Supplier Management** — Ensure supplier compliance with safety requirements

### Post-Market Phase

**Surveillance** — Monitor product performance and incidents  
**Complaint Handling** — Investigate and respond to consumer complaints  
**Recalls** — Rapid response to safety issues

## Product Recalls

**Voluntary Recalls** — Company-initiated removal of unsafe products  
**Mandatory Recalls** — Regulator-ordered recalls  
**Recall Effectiveness** — Measure % of products successfully removed from market

## Emerging Issues

**Chemical Safety** — REACH (EU), TSCA (US), California Prop 65  
**Cybersecurity** — Connected products and data security  
**Nanotechnology** — Novel materials with unknown long-term effects
