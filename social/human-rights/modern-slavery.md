# Modern Slavery & Forced Labor

**Modern slavery** encompasses forced labor, debt bondage, human trafficking, and slavery-like practices. An estimated **50 million people** are in modern slavery globally.

## Forms of Modern Slavery

**Forced Labor** — Work or service extracted under threat of penalty  
**Debt Bondage** — Labor to repay debt with exploitative terms  
**Human Trafficking** — Recruitment and movement for exploitation  
**Child Labor** — Work that deprives children of childhood and education

## High-Risk Sectors and Regions

**Sectors:** Agriculture, garment manufacturing, construction, mining, domestic work  
**Regions:** South Asia, Sub-Saharan Africa, Southeast Asia, Middle East

## Regulatory Requirements

**UK Modern Slavery Act** — Annual statements for companies >£36M turnover  
**Australia Modern Slavery Act** — Annual statements for companies >A$100M revenue  
**California Transparency in Supply Chains Act** — Disclosure of efforts to eradicate slavery

## Prevention Measures

**Supplier Audits** — Independent verification of working conditions  
**Worker Voice Mechanisms** — Grievance channels and worker interviews  
**Responsible Recruitment** — No recruitment fees charged to workers  
**Living Wages** — Wages sufficient to meet basic needs
