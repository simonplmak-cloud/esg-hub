# Mental Health Support

## Overview

Mental Health Support is an essential component of social sustainability and responsible business practices.

## Key Principles

- **Respect** — Honor stakeholder rights and perspectives
- **Inclusion** — Ensure equitable access and participation
- **Transparency** — Open communication and accountability
- **Continuous Improvement** — Regular assessment and enhancement

## Business Benefits

- Enhanced reputation and stakeholder trust
- Improved employee retention and productivity
- Reduced operational and reputational risks
- Better alignment with investor expectations

## Implementation Guidance

**Policy Development** — Establish clear commitments and procedures  
**Training & Awareness** — Build organizational capacity  
**Monitoring & Evaluation** — Track progress and effectiveness  
**Reporting** — Communicate performance transparently

## Measurement & Reporting

Report on mental health support using relevant GRI Standards indicators and other recognized frameworks.
