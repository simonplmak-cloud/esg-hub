# GRESB

**GRESB** assesses and benchmarks the ESG performance of real estate and infrastructure portfolios. Over **2,000 participants** represent **$7.1 trillion in assets**.

## Framework Overview

GRESB assesses performance across Management, Performance, Risk Management, Stakeholder Engagement, Building Certifications, New Construction, and Tenant Engagement. GRESB provides scores (0-100) and star ratings (1-5 stars) for peer group benchmarking.

## Why GRESB Matters

GRESB is the **dominant ESG benchmark** for real estate investors. Many institutional investors require portfolio companies to participate in GRESB and use scores for investment decisions.
