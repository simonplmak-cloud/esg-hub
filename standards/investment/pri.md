# PRI

The **UN Principles for Responsible Investment (PRI)** provides a framework for incorporating ESG factors into investment decision-making. Over **5,300 signatories** with **$120 trillion in assets** have committed to the six PRI principles.

## Six Principles

**Principle 1:** Incorporate ESG issues into investment analysis  
**Principle 2:** Be active owners and incorporate ESG into ownership policies  
**Principle 3:** Seek appropriate disclosure on ESG issues  
**Principle 4:** Promote acceptance of the Principles  
**Principle 5:** Work together to enhance effectiveness  
**Principle 6:** Report on activities and progress

## Why PRI Matters

PRI signatories commit to annual reporting, creating transparency and accountability in responsible investment. The PRI Reporting Framework aligns with TCFD.
