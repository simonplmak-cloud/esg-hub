# ISO 50001

**ISO 50001:2018** specifies requirements for establishing an energy management system (EnMS). Organizations achieve **average energy cost savings of 10-20%** within 3 years.

## Framework Overview

ISO 50001 follows the **Plan-Do-Check-Act** framework with specific focus on energy performance improvement through energy reviews, baselines, and performance indicators.

## Why ISO 50001 Matters

The standard supports compliance with energy efficiency regulations and demonstrates commitment to reducing operational emissions (Scope 1 & 2).
