# SBTN

The **Science Based Targets Network (SBTN)** provides methods for companies to set science-based targets for nature, complementing SBTi's climate targets. SBTN's **AR3T framework** (Avoid, Reduce, Regenerate & Restore, Transform) guides corporate action on nature across freshwater, land, ocean, and biodiversity.

## Framework Overview

SBTN targets address **five environmental systems**: Climate (via SBTi), Freshwater, Land, Ocean, and Biodiversity. Companies follow a **5-step process**:

**Reduce** â€” Minimize ongoing negative impacts

## Why SBTN Matters

SBTN targets enable companies to address the **nature crisis** with the same rigor as climate targets. With **1 million species at risk of extinction** and ecosystem degradation accelerating, SBTN provides the methodology for corporate nature action aligned with planetary boundaries.
