About This Hub

This resource hub is maintained by Simon Mak and Ascent Partners Foundation, a Hong Kong-based charity dedicated to advancing ESG education and sustainable finance.

Why Academic Frameworks?

  • Scientific Rigor: Grounded in peer-reviewed research
  • Comprehensive Coverage: Captures all material ESG topics
  • Clear Organization: Easy to navigate and understand
  • Alignment with Standards: Maps to major ESG reporting frameworks
