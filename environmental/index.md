# Environmental (E)

**Framework**: 9 Planetary Boundaries (Stockholm Resilience Centre)

---

## About the Framework

The **Planetary Boundaries** framework defines safe operating space for humanity by identifying nine critical Earth system processes. Transgressing these boundaries increases risk of triggering abrupt or irreversible environmental changes.

**Source**: Rockström et al. (2009), updated Steffen et al. (2015), Richardson et al. (2023)

**Primary Research**: [Stockholm Resilience Centre](https://www.stockholmresilience.org/research/planetary-boundaries.html)

---

## The 9 Planetary Boundaries

### [1. Climate Change](/environmental/climate-change/)
**Status**: Boundary transgressed  
**Metric**: Atmospheric CO₂ concentration, radiative forcing  
**Safe limit**: 350 ppm CO₂ | Current: ~420 ppm

### [2. Biodiversity Loss](/environmental/biodiversity/)
**Status**: Boundary transgressed  
**Metric**: Extinction rate  
**Safe limit**: <10 extinctions per million species-years | Current: 100-1000

### [3. Land-System Change](/environmental/land-system/)
**Status**: Boundary transgressed  
**Metric**: % of global land surface converted to cropland  
**Safe limit**: 75% of original forest cover | Current: 62%

### [4. Freshwater Use](/environmental/freshwater/)
**Status**: Within boundary (global), transgressed regionally  
**Metric**: km³/year of consumptive blue water use  
**Safe limit**: 4,000 km³/year | Current: ~2,600 km³/year globally

### [5. Biogeochemical Flows](/environmental/biogeochemical/)
**Status**: Boundary transgressed  
**Metric**: Nitrogen/phosphorus flows to biosphere  
**Safe limit**: 62 Tg N/year | Current: ~150 Tg N/year

### [6. Ocean Acidification](/environmental/ocean-acidification/)
**Status**: Within boundary  
**Metric**: Aragonite saturation state  
**Safe limit**: ≥80% of pre-industrial | Current: ~84%

### [7. Atmospheric Aerosol Loading](/environmental/aerosol-loading/)
**Status**: Uncertain (regional transgression)  
**Metric**: Aerosol optical depth  
**Safe limit**: Regional thresholds vary

### [8. Stratospheric Ozone Depletion](/environmental/ozone-depletion/)
**Status**: Within boundary (recovering)  
**Metric**: Stratospheric ozone concentration  
**Safe limit**: <5% reduction from pre-industrial | Current: recovering

### [9. Novel Entities](/environmental/novel-entities/)
**Status**: Boundary transgressed  
**Metric**: Concentration of synthetic chemicals, plastics, GMOs  
**Safe limit**: Not yet quantified
