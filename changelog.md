# ESG Standards Update Log

**Track major revisions to international ESG frameworks**

---

## 2026

### January 2026
**IFRS S1 & S2** — First wave of ISSB adoption reports published.

**SBTi Net-Zero Standard v2.0** — Updated sectoral pathways for 1.5°C alignment.

---

## 2025

### December 2025
**EU CSRD/ESRS Omnibus** — Simplified reporting for first-time adopters.

### September 2025
**HKEX ESG Code** — Enhanced climate disclosure aligned with IFRS S2.

### June 2025
**PCAF v3.0** — Expanded financed emissions guidance.

### March 2025
**GRI Universal Standards** — Minor updates for ISSB interoperability.

---

## 2024

### November 2024
**China CSDS** — China Sustainability Disclosure Standards issued, aligned with ISSB.

### September 2024
**Japan SSBJ** — Japanese sustainability standards finalized.

### June 2024
**IFRS S1 & S2** — ISSB standards issued, effective 1 January 2024.

### January 2024
**EU CSRD** — Corporate Sustainability Reporting Directive enters into force.

---

## 2023

### September 2023
**G20/OECD Principles** — Major revision adding Chapter VI on sustainability.

**TNFD v1.0** — Final recommendations released.

### July 2023
**EU ESRS Set 1** — 12 ESRS standards published.

### June 2023
**ISSB Standards Finalized** — IFRS S1 and S2 issued.

---

## 2022

### November 2022
**ILO Declaration** — Fifth fundamental principle added: safe and healthy working conditions.

---

## 2021

### November 2021
**GRI Universal Standards 2021** — Major revision: GRI 1, 2, 3.
