# SDG 12: Responsible Consumption

**Goal:** Ensure sustainable consumption patterns

## Business Relevance

Circular economy, waste reduction, sustainable products

## How Companies Contribute

Companies can advance SDG 12 through:
- Core business operations and products/services
- Supply chain management and procurement
- Employee programs and workplace policies
- Community investment and partnerships
- Advocacy and public policy engagement

## Measurement and Reporting

Track progress using relevant indicators from the **SDG Compass** and report contributions in sustainability reports aligned with GRI Standards.
