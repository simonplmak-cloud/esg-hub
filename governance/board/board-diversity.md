# Board Diversity

## Overview

Board Diversity is a fundamental element of corporate governance and long-term value creation.

## Key Principles

- **Accountability** — Clear roles, responsibilities, and oversight
- **Transparency** — Open disclosure and stakeholder communication
- **Independence** — Objective judgment and decision-making
- **Integrity** — Ethical conduct and compliance with laws

## Best Practices

**Governance Framework** — Establish clear policies and procedures  
**Oversight Mechanisms** — Implement effective monitoring and controls  
**Stakeholder Engagement** — Consider diverse perspectives  
**Continuous Improvement** — Regular review and enhancement

## Regulatory Requirements

Compliance with relevant corporate governance codes, securities regulations, and listing requirements.

## Reporting & Disclosure

Disclose board diversity practices in annual reports, corporate governance statements, and ESG reports.
